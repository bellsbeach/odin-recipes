Peanut Butter and Jelly Pie

Home

Description

This is a recipe from Seattle's High 5 Pie, a pie shop that used to be in the Capitol Hill neighborhood. I remember going there after soccer practice with my mom and best friend. YUM!

The peanut butter layer has a rich, but not too sweet mixture of peanut butter, cream cheese, sugar, milk, honey and a pinch of salt. Then for the jelly layer, I simply used store bought strawberry jelly, but I think homemade jam or jelly would be amazing. And then it's topped with fresh whipping cream and a handful of chopped peanuts.

Ingredients

  • pie crust, single
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 2/3 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup jelly, your fav flavor
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2-4 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • peanuts and fruit for garnish, optional

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, cream together peanut butter, cream cheese, sugar, milk, honey and salt until well combined
  2. Spoon into crust and spread evenly
  3. Add jelly to small bowl, and mix with a spoon or fork slightly to make it easier to spread
  4. Add to peanut butter layer and spread evenly
  5. Add cold heavy whipping cream to a large chilled bowl and beat on medium speed until cream begins to thicken
  6. Add vanilla and turn mixture to high, continue beating on high speed until cream thickens
  7. Gradually add powdered sugar until cream forms stiff peaks
  8. Generously spoon onto jelly layer
  9. Top with chopped peanuts and/or fruit
